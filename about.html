About This Project

My name is Phil Lawson. Since is was a child, I have suffered from Crohns' disease... or some close relative thereof, since no doctor could ever provide me with a straight answer as to what was wrong with my health. So, starting slowly at first in 2010, then all-out by 2013, I began searching high and low for the knowledge to determine 1) what made me feel so terrible all the time 2) what caused such a malfunction within me in the first place, and 3) how to fix it. Several months of navigating the dark alleyways of the Internet yielded an absolute treasure trove of knowledge on the internet, relating not just to my specific primary malady, but to all of my health-related maladies throughout life, those of my family and friends, and to multiple levels of function in the human physiology. With the help of one excellent physician, and of many of the supplements listed on this site, I have made a near-100% recovery, and still have a large backlog of projects which I steadily work through toward a vital, adamantine condition of health. This site executes a rough, simplified, automatic facsimile of my own method of diagnosis, selecting three supplements from some of my favorite brands to address the user's selected health maladies.
